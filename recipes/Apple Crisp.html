Apple Crisp

Description

This top-rated apple crisp recipe with a sweet oat topping is sure to satisfy everyone at your table. Use whatever all-purpose apples you prefer. Sweet Honeycrisp or Fuji apples work a treat, and crisp Granny Smith apples will keep their shape perfectly.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugars
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Oats
  • Leaveners
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place the apples in a baking dish.
  2. Sprinkle the apples with white sugar, flour, and cinnamon.
  3. Drizzle water over the apples.
  4. Make the oat topping, then sprinkle it over the apples.
  5. Bake until the topping is golden brown and the apple mixture is bubbly.
